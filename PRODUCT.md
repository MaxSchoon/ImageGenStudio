# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Creators and operators who generate polished visual assets for social content, especially LinkedIn creator workflows. They are usually iterating on prompts, production formats, and reference images while waiting on external model providers.

## Product Purpose

Image Generation Studio helps users turn prompts, briefs, and reference images into finished image assets across multiple AI models. Success means the user can choose the right model and format, start generation confidently, understand what the system is doing while they wait, and export a correctly sized result.

## Brand Personality

Focused, technical, and editorial. The interface should feel like a production tool for creator work, not a marketing page or a toy generator.

## Anti-references

Avoid generic SaaS landing-page visuals, decorative animations with no state meaning, vague AI assistant copy, and loading screens that feel stalled or artificially rushed.

## Design Principles

- Keep generation controls dense, legible, and task-first.
- Treat export dimensions and workflow presets as part of the product promise.
- Use loading states to explain progress and reduce uncertainty without pretending to know provider timing exactly.
- Preserve the dark studio shell and restrained accent vocabulary already established in the app.
- Make LinkedIn storybook workflows feel production-ready, with page-oriented language and consistent output expectations.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Respect reduced-motion preferences, keep text contrast readable on the dark studio palette, and avoid relying on color alone for progress or status.
